We may need to understand Italian when we want to:

1\. Buy some food: “I want…”/recognise Italian names of foods and ingredients,
2. Find the way: transport/buy tickets/road names,
3. Greetings and politeness,
4. Understand certain signs and instructions.

Therefore, we need not understand the grammar or vocabulary in full generality or completeness.
